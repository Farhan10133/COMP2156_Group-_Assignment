This file will contain details about important of learning DevOps engineer in software development cycle.
1. Faster Time-to-Market: Software development and deployment may be completed fast and effectively with the help of DevOps. Software updates may be distributed regularly thanks to continuous integration and delivery, allowing businesses to keep up with the changing needs of their customers and the demands of the market.
2. Improved Collaboration: DevOps brings together development and operations teams, breaking down traditional silos and promoting cross-functional collaboration. This allows teams to work together more effectively, with a shared understanding of the business objectives and customer needs.
